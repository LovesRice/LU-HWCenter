Lehigh University Health & Wellness Center

Supporting Student Health, Safety, and Well-Being

Services Provided

General Medical

  • Allergies, cold, cough, flu
  • Sore throat, earache
  • Infections & sinus pain
  • Gastrointestinal illness

Injury Care

  • Wound care
  • Sprains & strains
  • Splinter & suture removal
  • Concussion evaluation

Diagnostic Testing

  • Rapid strep test
  • Urinalysis
  • Pregnancy testing
  • Blood work (HNL)

Vaccinations

  • HPV (Gardasil)
  • Meningitis A, C, Y, W
  • Meningitis B (Bexsero & Trumenba)
  • Flu shot, Tetanus/Tdap

Women's Health

  • Gynecological exams
  • Contraceptive management
  • STI testing & treatment
  • Emergency contraception (Plan B)

Additional Support

  • Free condoms
  • Sexual assault support
  • Nutrition counseling
  • Temporary stay beds (2)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Where is the Health and Wellness Center?

Located in Johnson Hall, 36 University Dr, Bethlehem, PA 18015.

Does the Health and Wellness Center allow walk-ins?

No. The HWC is appointment‑based. To schedule, call 610‑758‑3870.

When is the Health and Wellness Center open?

Hours can be found on the official website or by calling the office.

Does Lehigh offer health insurance?

Yes. All eligible students must have health insurance. Students may enroll in Lehigh’s Student Health Insurance Plan or maintain their own plan if it meets requirements through August 14, 2026.

What if we can’t make it to an appointment?

The HWC has a strict no‑show, late, and cancellation policy. Fees may apply.
